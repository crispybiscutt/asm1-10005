I lived in Hanoi, which is the capital of VietNam. With a soul of a child born and raised in Hanoi, I have special feeling for this place. Hanoi lingered in me so many beautiful images of ancient architecture and the kindness of Hanoian. Otherwise, street vender is also a vital factor that create the traditional culture of Hanoi.

Hanoi, the capital city of Vietnam, is a captivating blend of ancient history, colonial architecture, and bustling modernity. Situated in the northern part of the country, along the banks of the Red River, Hanoi is a city steeped in tradition yet pulsating with energy.

About Hanoi:

  • Capital of Vietnam: Hanoi, the capital city of Vietnam, was founded in 1010 AD.
    • Key Attractions:
    • Old Quarter: Historic area with narrow streets and bustling markets.
    • Hoan Kiem Lake: Iconic lake with Ngoc Son Temple.
    • Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum: Final resting place of Ho Chi Minh.
    • Temple of Literature: Vietnam's first national university.
    • Hoa Lo Prison: Known as the "Hanoi Hilton."
  • Cuisine:
    • Pho: Famous noodle soup.
    • Bun Cha: Grilled pork with rice noodles.
    • Egg Coffee: Unique coffee with egg yolk.
  • Lifestyle:
    • Cyclo Tours: Explore the Old Quarter by cyclo.
    • Street Markets: Vibrant markets like Dong Xuan Market.
